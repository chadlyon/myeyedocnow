About Dr. Cho

Dr. Dianna Cho graduated from Emory University with a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology and Women's Studies. She graduated from Pennsylvania College of Optometry (Salus University) with a Bachelor of Science and Doctor of Optometry. Dr. Cho is National Board Certified in Optometry and received Honors in Clinical Excellence in Primary Care at The Eye Institute and Clinical Excellence in Low Vision at the Feinbloom Vision Rehabilitation Center. She is also Glaucoma and Therapeutic Certified and a member of the National Glaucoma Society. Dr. Cho has extensive experience in preoperative and postoperative co- management of refractive surgeries and specializes in the diagnoses and customized treatment of Dry Eye and Ocular Surface Disease. She provides her patients with overall ocular care in glaucoma, retina, and corneal disease. As a primary eye care provider, her passion is offering her patients the highest level of service and the latest visual solution, through contact lenses, glasses or LASIK.
